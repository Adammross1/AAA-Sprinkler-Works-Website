Services

Broken and Leaking Sprinklers


A broken head prevents proper coverage for your lawn. We replace all broken or leaking heads, as well as check and adjust the coverage of each head.

Valve Repair and Replacement


A bad valve can allow water to leak through its station constantly. We repair and replace broken valves so that no water is wasted.

Timer Repair


Setting the timer correctly is crucial for having green grass. We replace broken timers, as well as educate the customer on best practices for setting and using their timer.

Broken Pipes


Broken pipes waste water and reduce water pressure. We find and fix any leaking or broken pipes.

Broken Stop and Waste Valve


A broken stop and waste valve leaks constantly, wasting a lot of water. We repair and replace bad stop and waste valves.

Startups and Winterizations


We get sprinkler systems up and running for the Spring, repairing any leaks and fine-tuning coverage. In the Fall we blow out sprinkler lines to prevent freeze-breaks in the Winter.
